Videoclipper

Videoclipper is a prototype designed during a 3 months summer internship at the French institute for research in computer science and automation (INRIA). Working with HCI leading experts I designed a mobile app for structured video capture used by researchers and designers. The application is intended to aid shooting interviews, video-prototypes, tutorials and experiments.

After several iterations with low fidelity prototypes the application was implemented in record time on a software prototype used for user testing on Ipads.
